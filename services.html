Our Core Services

End-to-end solutions to maximize the performance and lifespan of your thermal equipment.

Custom Manufacturing & Design

We provide bespoke solutions, from initial thermal design to final fabrication. Our engineers work with you to build heat exchangers (Shell & Tube, Plate, Finned Coils) that are perfectly matched to your process. We build to all major codes, including TEMA and ASME, in our state-of-the-art facility.

Learn About Manufacturing

Expert Retubing & Re-bundling

Restore peak performance and extend the life of your existing equipment. Our specialists remove old, corroded, or damaged tubes and replace them with high-quality materials, including Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Copper, and Admiralty Brass. We can retube on-site or in our workshop.

Request Retubing Service

In-Situ (On-Site) Cleaning

Fouling and scaling are the #1 cause of heat exchanger inefficiency. Our advanced on-site chemical cleaning services dissolve tough deposits, remove blockages, and restore thermal efficiency, all while minimizing your operational downtime. We handle everything from setup to safe disposal.

Schedule a Cleaning

On-Site Repair & Maintenance

When a failure happens, you need a fast response. Our certified technicians provide on-site emergency repairs, including leak detection, tube plugging, gasket replacement, and weld repairs. We offer annual maintenance contracts to keep your plant running smoothly and prevent costly unplanned shutdowns.
