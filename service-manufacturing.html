Custom Heat Exchanger Manufacturing

From design and engineering to fabrication and testing, built to your exact specifications and industry codes.

At Narmada Engineering, we don't just supply heat exchangers; we engineer solutions. Our **custom manufacturing service** is built on a foundation of deep technical expertise and a state-of-the-art fabrication facility.

Our Proven Process

We follow a rigorous, step-by-step process to ensure quality and precision from start to finish:

  1. Consultation & Design: We work with you to understand your operational parameters. Our engineers use advanced thermal design software to create an optimal solution.
  2. Engineering & Approval: We provide detailed mechanical drawings and thermal data sheets for your review, ensuring compliance with all necessary codes (e.g., TEMA, ASME).
  3. Procurement & Fabrication: We source only high-quality materials (Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Copper, etc.). Our skilled fabricators then bring the design to life.
  4. Quality Assurance & Testing: Every unit undergoes rigorous testing, including hydrostatic pressure tests and dimensional checks, to guarantee leak-free, reliable performance.
  5. Delivery & Support: We ensure timely delivery and provide comprehensive documentation and after-sales support.
